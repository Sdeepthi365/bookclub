About BookClub

Connecting readers, sharing stories, and building community—one book at a time.

Our Mission

BookClub was founded to bring readers together. Our mission is to make reading more social, inclusive, and fun by creating a place where people can share stories, discover new authors, and connect with others.

What We Do

  • Create and join clubs organized by genre, author, or theme.
  • Track reading lists and share recommendations with friends.
  • Host discussions, polls, and meetups—online or in person.

Our Values

We believe in accessibility, respectful discussion, and lifelong learning. Whether you’re a casual reader or a literature lover, there’s a place for you at BookClub.

Meet the Team

We’re a small group of readers, designers, and developers who care about thoughtful design and community-driven features. Want to collaborate or share feedback? Contact us.
